PRIVACY POLICY version 1.1

Your location and all content that you post to the App and through its services, including photo uploads and comments, are publicly and visibly associated with the Facebook profile of your account; that content and profile can be viewed by anyone.

The App's services log information about each user that connects to them, in order to accurately maintain operability of the app on your device, and connect you to other active users. The information collected may include:
  • IP addresses;
  • Dates and times of each login;
  • Latitude and longitude;
  • Facebook profile information;
  • Device characteristics;
  • Operating system;
  • Browser type;
  • Connection type;
  • Page and image viewing statistics; and
  • Incoming and outgoing links.
We may use cookies, web beacons, or other anonymous tracking information to improve our services.

We collect the email address, first name, last name, and a unique application-scoped ID from users when they authorize Facebook to allow access to the App. We may send you promotional messages once in awhile to keep you apprised of Party activities; we'll try to make them fun and interesting but you can of course opt out at any time.
