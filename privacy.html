Privacy Policy

Your location and all content posted by an account to the App's services, including photos and comments, is publicly and visibly associated with the Facebook profile of that account, and that content and profile can be viewed by anyone.

The App's services logs information about each user connecting to them which may include IP address, dates and times of each login, latitude and longitude, facebook profile information, device characteristics, operating system, browser type, type of connection, page and image viewing statistics, and incoming and outgoing links.

We may revise our privacy policy from time to time by posting the changes here.
